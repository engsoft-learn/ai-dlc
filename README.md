## 📖 What is AI-DLC?

**AI-DLC** (**AI-Driven Development Life Cycle**) is a methodology that directs AI agents throughout the software development life cycle (SDLC) — from business intent to production — keeping the human in control. It operates as a structured workflow with phases and artifacts, avoiding the pitfalls of unguided "vibe-coding" and providing engineering discipline to AI-assisted coding.
